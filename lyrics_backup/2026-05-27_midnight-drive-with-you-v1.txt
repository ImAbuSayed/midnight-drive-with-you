[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows dancing on the radio dial
Got your hand resting close to mine
No destination but the open mile
I just want to lose track of time

[Verse 1]
The coffee is cold but the air is warm
Hear the gentle whisper of the tires hum
Safe with me inside this quiet storm
Knowing that the best is yet to come

[Pre-Chorus]
Every look you give me pulls me deep
Stealing breaths I didn't know I would keep
Windows down, the breeze is soft and slow
Tell me everywhere you want to go

[Chorus]
It is a midnight drive with you
Painting all the dusty roads in blue
Nothing but the stars and sweet desire
Setting my entire soul on fire

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, just you and me on this midnight drive
Making me feel so alive

[Verse 2]
Pulling over by the riverside
Counting constellations in your eyes
Nothing in this universe to hide
Every touch is a beautiful surprise

[Bridge]
Lost inside this midnight dream with you
Leaving an eternal kind of mark

[Spoken]
